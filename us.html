Who are we?

Who are we?

We are a group of veterans and active duty Sailors who have banded together from various rates and different commands. We are Aviation Structural Mechanic (AM), Aviation Machinist's Mates (AD), Aviation Structural Mechanic (AME), Aviation Electronics Technician (AT), Aviation Ordnanceman (AO), Aircrew Survival Equipmentman (PR), Hospital Corpsman (HM), Aviation Support Equipment Technicians (AS), U.S. Marine (MOS 3501), and some of still find time to continue our education in the local colleges. Our players are from different states across the nation and overseas. We are attached to different commands here on NAS Lemoore to defend our country, families, and civilians. What brings us together is our love for the sport, and to raise awareness for our fallen brothers,to those who feel alone. If you ever feel like you need help please reach out to someone. There are many avenues that can help you. You are not alone!

Resources

If you or know someone who needs help, ask the tough question or reach out. There are many resources available to all Military personnel, Active, Reserve, or any veteran. Military Onesource is a great start for anyone seeking help.
