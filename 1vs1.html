Biography

I am currently serving in the US Navy and have been for 8 years. I have been stationed in a few different states and have traveled quite a bit. My favorite place that I have been to is Philippines, followed by Japan as a very close second.

My Job

About the job: My job requires me to work on F18E/F engines and engine components. Trouble shooting, removing and and replacing, schedule and unschedule maintenance are a very busy part of my day. I find it more enjoyable when I encounter a new problem that I have not faced before. Being able to diagnose an issue and correcting it feels satisfying after seeing an aircraft back in the air.
